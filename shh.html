FAQ

  • What constitutes a "weird game"? I'll have on tap such classics as Katamari Damacy, Baba is You, Spaceteam the card game, and the amazing and amazingly obscure Pony Island. But weird is subjective, so bring whatever!
  • Can it be a game I made myself? Yes. Please. Obviously. Yes. That would be amazing.
  • What systems does this party support? I've got Windows, Linux, Switch, and PS4(+VR). Feel free to bring whatever extra tech you might need (an extra monitor might not be a bad idea...). Games that don't require computers are of course also welcome.
  • Can I bring a game that uses real fire? ... no.
  • But I don't have any weird games! That's fine, just bring yourself!
  • Do I have to play? No, it's totally cool if you just come to hang and/or watch other people play. I'm going for more "arcade" and less "game night" here.
  • What's the food/drink situation? There will be both! Drinks will be both alcoholic and not. Let me know your dietary preferences when you RSVP.
  • I want to hang but parties stress me out! HMU, we'll figure something out.

RSVP via twitter, instagram, PIGSlack, in person, carrier pigeon, whatever, just let me know you're coming!
