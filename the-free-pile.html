FAQ

What is this? Is this an escape room?

Sort of. It's half about solving puzzles and half about uncovering the story: what happened to the previous tenant?

It is intended to be a spooky experience, but it's not any kind of haunted house. There are no jumpscares or anything like that.

Where exactly will it take place?

In an actual apartment. Location details will be emailed to you after you reserve a time slot.

How risky is this, covid-wise?

This experimence runs for only one party at a time, in an actual apartment. You may share the elevator with another resident or two, but likely you will not interact with any other people, except the host, who is vaccinated and boosted. (And if you'd prefer to minimize contact with the host, that can be arranged.)

I can't make it in person! Will there be any kind of virtual version, or future run?

The Free Pile will probably not run again. But I am planning to do a crowd-controlled stream for my patreon supporters!
